Frequently Asked Questions

Discover answers to common queries about AnimeXpress with our FAQs, covering topics from account setup to technical support for a seamless anime experience.

1. Is AnimeXpress free to use?

We offer premium subscription plans. The free plan includes access to a limited library with ads, while the premium plan offers an ad-free experience with access to our full catalog.

2. What devices are compatible with AnimeXpress?

AnimeXpress is compatible with most devices, including smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, and web browsers. Check our "Device Compatibility" page for more details.

3. Can I watch anime offline?

Yes, our premium members can download episodes to watch offline. Simply click the download button on the episode page

4. Are subtitles available for all episodes?

Yes, we provide subtitles for most of our episodes. You can select your preferred language in the video player settings.

5. How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime by going to the "Subscription" section in your account settings and selecting "Cancel Subscription.

6. How do I create an account on AnimeXpress?

Creating an account is easy. Just click on the "Sign Up" button and follow the instructions to register with your email or link your Gmail account.

7. How do I report a problem with a video?

If you encounter any issues with a video, please use the "Report a Problem" feature located below the video player, or contact our support team.

Most Watched Anime

Discover the most-watched anime on AnimeXpress! Dive into popular series that captivate audiences worldwide with thrilling stories and unforgettable characters. Join the excitement and see why these shows are fan favorites!

chainsawman

Action, Thriller

Chainsaw Man follows Denji, a young man burdened with debt who becomes a devil hunter. After merging with his pet devil, Pochita, he gains the ability to transform parts of his body into chainsaws to battle dangerous devils.

One piece

Action, Adventure

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they journey across the Grand Line in search of the legendary One Piece treasure, aiming to become the Pirate King. Along the way, they face formidable enemies, uncover secrets, and forge powerful bonds of friendship.

Naruto

Shonen, Adventure

Naruto follows Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and leader of his village. Along his journey, he faces numerous challenges and forms powerful bonds, uncovering the true meaning of friendship and perseverance.

Death Note

Suspense, Thriller

Death Note follows Light Yagami, a high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook that grants him the power to kill anyone by writing their name in it. As he attempts to create a utopia by eliminating criminals, a brilliant detective named L challenges him in a high-stakes battle of wits.

Attack On Titan

Action, Dark Fantasy

Attack on Titan follows humanity's desperate struggle for survival against giant humanoid Titans. As they uncover shocking secrets about the Titans, they fight to reclaim their world.
